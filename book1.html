Mixed Media Photozine
This was a final I did for a digital photography class and it ended up being one of my favorite pieces of work I've ever done and I'd like to talk about the creative process for this photozine. I received a $45 dollar digital camera from my professor and I had just used it to take photos of me spending time with my friends, I would then take these photos into Photoshop and rasterize them to make them black and white bitmaps which gave the photos a really nice grainy effect. I then hit them with a color overlay and tried to keep a consistent color per spread. I then printed these photos out onto different colored sheets of 8x14 paper. I then added scribbles and doodles with pencils, ink pens, and gel pens. Finally once I finished I saddle stiched the pages together to make it a photozine.
8x14 (each spread)
